Viva Schools is located in the heart of Ikorodu, providing quality education to students from all income brackets. Our institution is built on excellence, discipline, and innovation to ensure every child succeeds and thrives in the future.

Our community is inclusive, supportive, and deeply rooted in the values that promote academic excellence, moral strength, and personal growth.
